Welcome to the Air Quality Page

Our page provides information about air quality and its impact on health.

What is Air Quality?

Air quality refers to the condition of the air around us. It can be affected by a variety of factors, including pollution, weather conditions, and natural events such as wildfires. Poor air quality can have a negative impact on our health and the environment.

Types of Air Pollutants

Air pollutants can be categorized into two main types:

  • Primary pollutants: These pollutants are emitted directly into the air by sources such as factories, vehicles, and power plants.
  • Secondary pollutants: These pollutants are formed in the air when primary pollutants react with other substances. Examples include ozone and sulfuric acid.

Health Effects of Poor Air Quality

Poor air quality can have a variety of negative health effects, including:

  • Respiratory problems such as asthma and bronchitis
  • Cardiovascular problems such as heart disease and stroke
  • Reproductive problems
  • Neurological problems
